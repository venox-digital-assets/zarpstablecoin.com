[ZARP Stablecoin](https://www.zarpstablecoin.com/) has launched with incentives and AERO emissions on [Aerodrome](https://aerodrome.finance/), the premier trading and liquidity marketplace on [Base](https://www.base.org/). This marks an exciting milestone as ZARP liquidity providers can now earn fees and additional rewards.

**Why This Matters**

- Deeper Liquidity: The new Aerodrome incentives encourage liquidity providers to stake their ZARP pairs, boosting on-chain liquidity and enhancing stability for ZARP users.
- Rewards: Liquidity providers who stake in the ZARP gauge earn AERO emissions.
- Potential for incentives: veAERO voters receive trading fees and additional deposited incentives if added, maximising possible rewards.

**How to Participate**

- Add Liquidity: Head over to the [ZARP liquidity pools on Aerodrome](https://aerodrome.finance/liquidity?query=zarp) to view current APR opportunities. Deposit liquidity and start earning fees and rewards immediately.
- Get extra rewards: [Create a lock](https://aerodrome.finance/locks) to gain greater voting power and higher rewards.
- Vote: Contribute to Aerodrome's governance by [voting for ZARP](https://aerodrome.finance/vote?query=zarp) emissions, and earn even more rewards and incentives.

**Looking Ahead**

As we continue our mission to bring stable, accessible financial tools to the crypto ecosystem, this collaboration with Aerodrome is a key step forward. ZARP Stablecoin's integration into the Aerodrome incentives program will not only boost liquidity but also expand the reach and utility of ZARP on the Base network.
